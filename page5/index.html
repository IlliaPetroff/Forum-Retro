    • COLLECTING

    Collecting retro cars is a popular hobby that combines a passion for automotive design and
    history. Collectors often seek rare models or cars with cultural or historical significance. They
    may restore, preserve, or modify these vehicles, with a focus on originality. The most valuable
    cars are often limited editions or those with a notable place in pop culture or cinema. Many
    collectors also participate in exhibitions and auctions to buy or sell unique cars.

    RESTORING

    Restoring retro cars involves repairing and preserving vintage vehicles to their original condition
    or enhancing performance. This includes refurbishing engines, restoring bodywork, and sourcing
    original parts. Restorations often focus on maintaining authenticity, using period-correct
    materials, though some may include modern upgrades. It’s a detailed process requiring
    knowledge and a passion for preserving automotive history.
