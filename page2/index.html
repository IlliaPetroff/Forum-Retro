    • The History of Iconic
    Retro Cars

    The Birth of the Automobile (Late
    19th - Early 20th Century)
    The history of retro cars began
    with Karl Benz’s Patent-
    Motorwagen in 1886. In 1908,
    Henry Ford transformed the
    industry with the affordable Model
    T, making cars accessible to the
    masses.

    The Golden Age
    of Automobiles
    (1920s-1940s)
    In the 1920s-1940s,
    luxury brands like Rolls-
    Royce and Bugatti
    produced elegant
    vehicles. The Art Deco
    movement inspired sleek
    designs, such as the
    1936 Cord 810 and
    Chrysler Airflow.

    Post-War Boom (1950s-1960s)
    After World War II, iconic models
    like the Chevrolet Bel Air and
    Porsche 356 emerged. Muscle
    cars like the Ford Mustang
    became symbols of power and
    individuality, defining a new era
    of automotive culture.
