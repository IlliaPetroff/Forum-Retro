    • Aesthetics and Design
    of Retro Cars

    Retro cars are known for their bold
    and distinctive design. They often
    featured chrome accents on
    bumpers, grilles, and trim, adding a
    touch of luxury. Body shapes were
    characterized by sweeping curves and
    tail fins, like in the Cadillac Eldorado.
    Interiors boasted handcrafted
    materials such as wood and leather,
    with vibrant color schemes. Retro car
    design focused on style and
    individuality, making these vehicles
    iconic symbols of their time.
